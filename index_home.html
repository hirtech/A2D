Problem and Solution

Every year millions are spent to keep the mosquitoes from spreading deadly diseases, to keep our homes, workplaces and outdoors infestation and irritant free and making our farms be more productive from various pests to meet the world's growing food needs.

There are multiple Vector management operations to help keep us safe, our environment clean and crops healthy. Some of them are funded by taxpayers and others are private. Most of these operations are still handled on pen, paper and desktops without any data being shared among them.

With intuitive and easy to navigate web application with tons of cool innovative features developed by us, even small operations now have access to a world-class vector control system which make daily work and regulation-compliance simpler at a fraction of the cost.

Features

Our Al based geospatial pest management IT platform solves the stated problem with following features.

  • Mosquito surveillance management involving standardized trapping, species identification,abundance, and spatial distribution within a geographic area through the collection of eggs, larvae, and adult mosquitoes.
  • Treatment decisioning, planning and mapping based on surveillance data
  • Larviciding and Adulticiding applications (aerial and ground) management
  • Recording and reporting of pesticide usage and inventory management
  • Workforce and Fieldwork planning
  • Labwork management
  • Customer relationships management
  • Resource management including technicians, customers, administrators, pesticides and vehicles
  • Integration with other vector control programs
  • Integrated reporting module and automated report creation and delivery at regular bases.
  • Robust record keeping capabilities to ensure compliance and licensing with multiple government agencies like EPA and programs like NPDES
  • In-Field and Offline data capture and sync using handheld devices like iPhone, inad, android devices and toughbook
  • Automated notification with app alerts, emails and texts
  • Authentication based on state of the art secure protocols like 2FA and role based authorization
  • Portal for community outreach and education campaigns regarding mosquito borne diseases, how they spread, and how to prevent infection
  • Vector control activities management other than chemical control (e.g. biological, source reduction, catch basin treatment and water management )
  • Customized service request flow
  • Resource time keeping and billing based on it
  • Activity history based prediction with sophisticated Al algorithms
  • Optimal routing and navigation for daily field work.

User stories facilitated by this platform

  • As a department head, I would like to predict and manage the seasonal manpower requirement based on historical data.
  • As a department head, I would like to better manage resources(manpower, chemical, equipments etc) for day to day field operations.

  • As a department head, I would like to manage peak days efficiently in terms of service requests without staff getting overwhelmed.

  • As a department head, I need a dashboard to see inspection results, treatment areas and treatment efficacy for last one week
  • As a department head, I need a better tools to engage with community our department serves.
